Apple pie recipe

Description

A classic! The apple pie, just like how grandma used to make it.
With apples, cinnamon and raisins.

Ingredients

  • 1 kilo apples
  • 450 grams flour
  • 250 gram butter
  • 250 gram light brown caster sugar
  • 100 grams raisins
  • 50 gram granulated sugar
  • 2 eggs
  • 3 tsp cinnamon
  • 1 tsp vanille extract
  • salt and lemon juice

Steps

  1. Place the raisins in a bowl of warm watter and let them soak for a while.
  2. Set the oven on 175 degrees celsius.
  3. Beat the 2 eggs in a small bowl.
  4. Mix the flour, butter, brown sugar, vanilla extract, a pinch of salt and about 1 1/2 eggs together to form a nice compact dough.
    (keep the other half of the egg aside so you can brush it over the apple pie later)
  5. Grease in the springform pan.
  6. Use about 3/4 of the douch to coved the base and the sides of the springform pan.
  7. Peel the apples and chop them small. Place them in a bowl and drizzle them with lemon juice.
    Drain the raisins and mix them with the apples, along with the granulated sugar and cinnamon.
  8. Fill your pie crust with the apple mixture.
  9. Use the remaining dough to create long strips and arrange them in a diamond pattern
    over your apple pie. Press the edges together.
  10. Brush the remaining egg over the top of the pie. Bake for about an hour,or until golden brown. Enjoy!
