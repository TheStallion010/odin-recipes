Teriyaki bowl recipe

Description

Easy and delicious 5 ingredient salmon teriyaki bowl

Ingredients

  • 4 salmon filets
  • 1/2 tsp salt
  • 1/2 cup teriyak sauce
  • 3 cups jasmine rice
  • sriracha sauce(optional)
  • 2 cups of shelled edamame
  • 1 avocado, peeled and diced

Steps

  1. Preheat the oven to 235 degrees celsius and line a rimmed baking sheet
    with foil and lightly grease the pan
  2. Season the salmon with salt and pepper and spread 1 tablespoon teriyaki sauce
    over each fillet. Set salmon on the prepared baking sheet.
  3. Bake in the preaheated oven until salmon flakes easily with a fork.
    This is usually between 10 to 12 minuted depending on the thickness of the salmon.
  4. Meanwhile prepare the rice and edamame according to the package. Keep it warm.
  5. Divide the rice among bowls and top with the edamame
    Flake 1 piece of salmon on top and add the avocado.
  6. Drizzle 1 to 2 tablespoons of the remaining teriyaki sauce over each bowl and
    drizzle with sriracha sauce.
